Color Scheme

Primary Color (2E387B)

This color will apply to the body and background of the page.

Secondary Color (F4D643)

This color will contrast the primary color and grab attention of users. The sun and moon in some of the icons will contain this color.

Accent Color 1 (577A3C)

This color will add detail and interst to the page in the background and detials of the page.

Accent Color 2 (EAF0F9)

This color will be used in the primapry color to make the test readable. Titles and heades will be this color.
