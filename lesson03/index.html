Site Name:

World of Weather

Site Purpose:

The purpose of this site is to provide infromation relating to the weather with a unique twist. The unique twist is a mystical, magical, quest theme. Weather is a prediction of the future, much like a scorsorer looking into a glass ball to predit the future. Users will come to the site seeking the weather of their local city. It will focus on recomending the right materials/clothes for the day based on the weather This will help them prepare for their day. Their day will be refered to as an adventure to make the site exciting and interesting.
